This is a remarkable rework on Chinese Chess game with the beautiful graphics and animation, nice sound and music, intelligent AI.

The game has features:

- 10 levels of difficulty in training mode.

- Undo

- Select which board look easily with 4 board types.

- Choose your favorite piece with 4 piece set types.

- Personalize your profile with 7 avatar types.

- Beatiful graphics and animations.

- Dynamic sound and comfortable music

- Support English and Vietnamese
